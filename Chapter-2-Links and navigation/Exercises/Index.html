Kung Fu Panda

Kung Fu Panda is a 2008 animated film that follows Po, a clumsy and overweight panda who dreams of becoming a kung fu master. When the opportunity arises to train as a legendary warrior, Po faces many challenges and learns the true meaning of inner peace.

Key Information

Genre: Animation, Action, Adventure

Directed by: John Stevenson, Mark Osborne

Release Date: June 6, 2008

Official Website

Cast

  • Jack Black as Po
  • Dustin Hoffman as Master Shifu
  • Angelina Jolie as Tigress
  • Jackie Chan as Monkey

Characters

Po: A clumsy panda who becomes the Dragon Warrior.

Shifu: A wise kung fu master who trains Po.

Tigress: A fierce and skilled kung fu fighter.
