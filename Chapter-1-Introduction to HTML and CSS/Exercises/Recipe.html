Farfalle Pasta

Farfalle pasta is a type of pasta that means "butterflies" in Italian—often referred to as "bow ties" in English. It's perfect for creamy or tomato-based sauces and works wonderfully in this simple pasta recipe with pancetta.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups unsalted butter
  • 250g farfalle pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken or mushrooms (optional)
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Heat butter or olive oil in a pan.
  3. Sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add cream and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Stir in Parmesan, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  6. Add the cooked pasta and mix well to coat.
  7. Add chicken, mushrooms, or tomatoes if using, and mix through.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley and serve warm.
