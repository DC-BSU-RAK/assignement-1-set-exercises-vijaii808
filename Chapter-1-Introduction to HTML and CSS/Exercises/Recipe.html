Farfalle Pasta

Farfalle pasta is a type of pasta shape which means 'butterflies' in Italian often know as 'bow ties' in English. It's used for both creamy and tomato based sauces and goes perfectly with this simple pasta with pancetta recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cup unsalted butter
  • 250g farfalle pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken or mushrooms (optional)
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Heat butter/oil in a pan.
  3. Sauté minced garlic.
  4. Add cream and simmer.
  5. Stir in Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  6. Mix in pasta.
  7. Add chicken, mushrooms, or tomatoes.
